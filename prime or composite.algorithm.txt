Algorithm to find the given number is prime or composite:
1.Start
2.Recieve a positive integer "n"(Number greater than two)
3.Intialize counter with the value two
4.Divide the number "n" with the counter value
5.If the remainder is zero, display that the given number "n" is composite
6.Else incriment the counter and repeat from the step four until the counter value is less than "n"
7.After the complition of all the steps display, the number "n" is prime
8.Stop
